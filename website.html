Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients like meats, vegetables, and herbs.

Burgers

Burgers are a popular food item that typically consists of a patty placed between two pieces of bread, often accompanied by vegetables, cheese, sauces, and condiments.
